About Me

Hi, I'm Billy. I'm 18 years of age, and I am a self-taught graphic designer from the Midwest U.S.A! I find graphic design to be extremely rewarding as it gives you real-world experience and teaches you valuable skills.

I specialize in graphic design such as police, fire, and EMS liveries, which are commonly used within Grand Theft Auto or similar communities. I can tackle pretty much any form of digital graphics!

I have a keen eye for position and detailing and am often a perfectionist in my craft. I am fully self-taught and love to learn. I also make music and play instruments for fun.
