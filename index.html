About Me

Hi, I'm Billy. I'm 18 and like changing games more than playing them at this point. I find graphic design to be extremely rewarding as it gives you real world experience and teaches you skills.

I specialize in graphic design such as police, fire and EMS liveries, which are commonly used within Grand Theft Auto, or similar. I can tackle pretty much any form of digital graphics!

I have a keen eye for position and detailing and am often a perfectionist in my craft. I am fully self taught and love to learn, I also make music and play instruments for fun.
